System Map

A description and explanation of the system map.

A systems map is used to connect different nodes and denote their relationship to one another. It gives the reader a clearer understanding of what is being illustrated. For the Tamagotchi device, many components play an integral role in defining their purpose and connection to other nodes. The algorithms, ranging from the programmers, gaming software, manufacturers, creators, marketers, users, and fandom communities allow patrons to realize the excitement that stems from this respective device. In addition, the systems map even gives programmers and manufacturers an improved strategy to accomplish their goals. Anyone examining the Tamagotchi systems map will be able to become cognizant of what makes sense and what does not.
